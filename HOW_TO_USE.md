# How to Use imprUv

**imprUv** helps you track training sessions, measure progress over time, and get coaching insights. The tagline says it well: *Every session, better than the last.*

This guide walks through each part of the app.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign in

When you first open imprUv, sign in with your **Apple ID**. This verifies your identity and allows you to enable Pro Subscription features. Your account info is stored securely on your device.

### 2. Pick a session type

After sign-in, you land on the **Session** tab — a grid of training templates (e.g. Climbing, Biking, Hiking, Weight Training). Tap one to start.

### 3. Log your work

Record activities as you go, then tap **End Session** when you're done.

### 4. Review your progress

Use the **Progress** and **History** tabs to see trends, insights, and past sessions.

---

## The Four Tabs

imprUv is organized around four tabs at the bottom of the screen:

| Tab | Icon | Purpose |
|-----|------|---------|
| **Session** | Grid | Start new sessions and log activities |

## Session Tab

The Session tab is your home base for active training.

### Choosing a template

Templates define what you track in a session — which activities to log and what metrics to record. Each template appears as a card with an icon and name.

Built-in templates include:

- **Climbing** 
- **Biking** 
- **Hiking** 
- **Weight Training** 

Tap a template card to open a new session.

### Starting a session

When a session starts:

1. **Session name** — Defaults to something like `Climbing, 6/26/25`. If you allow location access, imprUv may replace this with a venue name or address (e.g. `Planet Granite, 6/26/25`).
2. **Date** — Set the session date.
3. **Session fields** — Some templates include session-level options (e.g. Indoor vs. Outdoor for climbing).

### Logging activities

Scroll to **Recorded Activities** and tap **Add** to log an activity.

For each activity you can:

- Set the **date and time**
- Fill in **metric fields** — dropdowns, sliders, numbers, or checkboxes depending on the template
- Add **notes** (up to 500 characters) about how it went

Tap **Save** to add the activity to your session. Activities are **auto-saved** as you go.

**Multiple activity types:** Some templates support more than one activity type (e.g. Climbing and Bouldering). Use the **Add** menu to pick which type to log.

**Edit or delete:** Tap an activity to edit it. Swipe left on an activity to delete it.

### During an active session

**End Session** (bottom button) — Finishes the session. You'll be asked to rate your **effort** on a 1–10 scale before saving.

**Session Goals** (wand icon, top right) — *Pro feature.* Describe your goal for the day (e.g. "I want to push myself on harder routes") and get AI-powered activity recommendations based on your training history.

**Display Mode** (⋯ menu, top right) — *Pro feature.* Change how stats appear while you train:

- **Normal** — Shows session total and activity average scores
- **Focus Mode → Volume** — Emphasizes total session score
- **Focus Mode → Quality** — Emphasizes average activity score
- **Distraction Free** — Hides stats so you can focus on training

Free users see a simplified score preview with an option to upgrade.

### Ending a session

1. Tap **End Session**
2. Rate your **effort** (1 = easy, 10 = maximum)
3. Confirm to save

The session moves to **History** and feeds into **Progress** insights.

### Resuming a session

If you leave the app mid-session, simply go to the history tab and reopen your last session.

## Progress Tab

The Progress tab shows how you're improving over time, organized by session type.

### Progress overview

Each template you've used appears as a card showing a mini chart and recent performance. Tap a card to open detailed insights for that activity type.

If you haven't completed any sessions yet, you'll see a prompt to start your first one.

### Inside a template's insights

Each template's insight screen has several sections:

#### Trends

Tap to open detailed trend charts:

- **Volume Trend** — Total score across all activities in a session (how much work you did)
- **Quality Trend** — Average score per activity (how well you performed)

*Pro users* get full interactive trend charts. Free users see a simplified preview.

**Lag analysis** (*Pro*) — When your volume and quality trends diverge, imprUv detects the pattern and provides an AI explanation of what it means for your training.

#### AI Insights (*Pro*)

An AI-generated coaching summary based on your recent sessions — highlights, areas to improve, and suggested goals for your next session. Generated on demand when you open this section.

#### Last Session

A breakdown of your most recent session, including:

- Volume and quality scores vs. your averages
- Performance callouts (things that stood out)
- Time spent in the session

*Pro users* see percentage changes and more detailed comparisons.

### Free vs. Pro on Progress

| Feature | Free | Pro |
|---------|------|-----|
| Sessions shown in overview | Last 5 | Last 7 |
| Full trend charts | Preview only | Full charts |
| Lag analysis + AI explanation | — | ✓ |
| AI progress summary | — | ✓ |
| Detailed session comparisons | Limited | Full |

## History Tab

The History tab is your archive of completed sessions.

### Browsing history

Sessions are grouped by template type. Each row shows:

- Template name and icon
- Total session count
- Date of your most recent session

Tap a template to see all its sessions.

### Session list

Each session shows:

- Session name
- Total score
- Date and time

**Edit a session** — Tap any session to open it and change activities, notes, effort, or other details.

**Delete a session** — Swipe left and tap Delete. This cannot be undone.

The list loads more sessions automatically as you scroll.

## Understanding Your Scores

imprUv uses two main metrics:

### Volume (Session Total)

The combined score of all activities in a session. Higher volume = more total work logged (more routes, more reps, longer efforts, etc.).

### Quality (Activity Average)

The average score across individual activities. Higher quality = stronger performance per activity (harder grades, better completion, etc.).

Each field in a template contributes to these scores based on its weight and the value you select. For example, in Climbing, logging a harder grade or a Lead climb (vs. Top Rope) increases your score.

During a session, imprUv compares your current scores to your **last session** and your **recent average** (from your last several sessions) so you can see if you're improving in real time.

## imprUv Pro

Pro is a subscription that unlocks advanced features:

- Advanced activity metrics and live session stats
- AI session goals and activity recommendations
- AI progress summaries and coaching feedback
- Unlimited session history
- Detailed performance analytics and trend charts

### Subscribing

Pro upgrade screens appear when you tap a locked feature, or you can subscribe from the paywall when prompted. Choose **monthly** or **yearly**, then confirm with Face ID / Touch ID / your Apple ID password.

### Managing your subscription

- **Restore Purchases** — On the subscription screen, if you've already subscribed
- **Cancel** — iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → imprUv Pro

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of imprUv

1. **Log consistently** — Progress insights improve with more sessions. Even short sessions help build useful trends.

2. **Use notes** — Activity notes feed into AI insights. A quick note like "felt strong on overhangs" helps the AI give better coaching.

3. **Set session goals** (*Pro*) — Before you start, use Session Goals to get a focused plan for the day.

4. **Check Trends weekly** (*Pro*) — Volume vs. quality trends reveal whether you're pushing harder, performing better, or both.

5. **Allow location** — Optional, but it makes session names more meaningful when you're training at different venues.

6. **Review Last Session** — After each session, check how you compared to your averages before your next visit.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need internet to use imprUv?**  
Core session tracking works offline. Sign in with Apple, AI features, and subscriptions require internet.

**Where is my data stored?**  
On your device. imprUv does not sync your training history to a cloud server.

**Can I use imprUv for activities beyond the built-in templates?**  
The current release includes built-in templates for common activities. Custom template creation may be added in future updates.

**What happens if I delete the app?**  
Locally stored sessions and templates are removed. Restore from an iCloud or computer backup to recover data.

**I closed the app mid-session — is my data lost?**  
No. imprUv auto-saves as you log activities and will resume an incomplete session when you reopen the app.
