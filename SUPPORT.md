**General Troubleshooting**

**Why do I need to sign in with Apple?**

Sign in with Apple verifies your identity and enables Pro subscription features. Your Apple user ID is stored securely on your device.

**I signed in but my name or email is missing. Am I properly signed in?**

Apple only shares your name and email the first time you sign in to an app. If you hid your email or skipped sharing your name, imprUv may not have that information. This does not affect app functionality.

**How do I sign out?**

Sign out is available in the developer menu (if enabled). To fully revoke access:

Settings → Apple ID → Sign-In & Security → Apps Using Apple ID → imprUv → Stop Using Apple ID

This removes your stored credentials. Your training data remains on your device until you delete the app.

**I can't sign in**
- Make sure you're connected to the internet.
- Confirm Sign in with Apple is enabled for your Apple ID.
- Try restarting the app or your device.
- If the problem persists, contact support.

**Progress & AI Features (Pro)**

**How do AI features work?**

When you use AI features (session goals, progress summaries, lag analysis, or AI template creation), imprUv sends relevant training context — such as scores, activity names, dates, and notes you've written — to our AI service to generate a response. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**AI gave an unhelpful or incorrect response:**

AI suggestions are coaching aids, not professional advice. Try rephrasing your goal or prompt, or run the feature again. If you consistently see poor results, contact support with details about what you asked and what you received.

**AI features aren't loading**

- Confirm you have an active Pro subscription.
- Check your internet connection.
- Wait a moment and try again — AI requests can take a few seconds.
- Restart the app.
- Subscriptions & Billing

**What's included in Pro?**

Pro is a subscription that unlocks advanced analytics, unlimited history, and all AI-powered features. Free users have access to core session tracking.

**How do I subscribe?**

Open the Pro upgrade screen in the app and choose a monthly or yearly plan. Payment is processed securely through Apple.

**How do I restore my purchase?**

On the subscription screen, tap Restore Purchases. You can also go to:

iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions

and confirm imprUv Pro is active.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**

Subscriptions are managed through Apple, not inside imprUv:

- Open iOS Settings
- Tap your name at the top
- Tap Subscriptions
- Select imprUv Pro
- Tap Cancel Subscription
- You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

**I was charged but Pro isn't active**
- Tap Restore Purchases on the subscription screen.
- Confirm the subscription is active in iOS Settings → Subscriptions.
- Force-quit and reopen imprUv.
- If still not working, contact support with your Apple purchase receipt.

**Refund requests**
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly. We cannot process refunds on your behalf.

**Subscription renewal**
Subscriptions automatically renew unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your Apple ID is charged within 24 hours prior to renewal.
