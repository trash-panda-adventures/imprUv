**Privacy Policy for imprUv
Last updated: June 26, 2026**

Introduction
**imprUv** is a training progression app that helps you track sessions, measure performance, and get AI-powered insights. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when you use the imprUv mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

**Information We Collect**

**1. Account Information**
If you sign in with Apple, we receive:

- A unique Apple user identifier
- Your name (if you choose to share it)
- Your email address (if you choose to share it)
  
This information is stored securely on your device using the iOS Keychain. We do not operate our own account server.

**2. Training and Session Data**
When you use the App, you may create and store:

- Session names, dates, and notes
- Activity names, metrics, scores, and custom field values
- Custom training templates
- Labels and other preferences
  
This data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. We do not automatically upload your full training history to our servers.

**3. Location Information**
With your permission, the App may access your device’s location while you are using the App to suggest a venue name for new sessions (for example, a gym or climbing wall). Location is used only for this purpose. We convert your location to a place name and do not store precise GPS coordinates.

You can deny or revoke location access at any time in your device Settings.

**4. AI Feature Data (Pro Subscribers)**
If you use AI-powered features (such as session goals, progress summaries, lag analysis, or AI template creation), we send relevant context to our AI service provider to generate responses. This may include:

- Aggregated performance scores and metrics
- Activity names and field values
- Dates of past sessions
- Notes you have entered
- Goals or prompts you provide
- Your Apple user identifier may be included with these requests to help manage usage. Do not include sensitive personal information in session notes or AI prompts if you do not want that content processed by our AI provider.

**5. Subscription and Purchase Information**
Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. We may receive subscription status information from Apple to determine whether Pro features are active.

We log subscription-related events (such as paywall views and purchase outcomes) through our analytics service. Price information sent to analytics reflects the display price shown in the App Store.

**6. Analytics and Usage Data**
We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand how the App is used. This may include:

- Screen views
- Session start and completion events (including template name, duration, effort score, and activity count)
- Subscription and paywall interactions
- This data is used to improve the App and is not used for advertising or cross-app tracking.

**How We Use Your Information**
We use the information we collect to:

- Provide core App functionality (tracking sessions, templates, and insights)
- Authenticate you via Sign in with Apple
- Enable optional location-based session naming
- Deliver AI-powered coaching and insights for Pro subscribers
- Process and validate subscriptions
- Analyze App usage and improve features and reliability
- Respond to support requests

**Data Storage and Retention**
- On your device: Training data, templates, and preferences are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad.
- In the Keychain: Account credentials from Sign in with Apple are stored securely on your device.
- With third parties: Analytics and AI request data are retained according to each provider’s policies.
  
If you delete the App from your device, locally stored training data is removed unless you have a device backup that includes it.

Signing out (or revoking Sign in with Apple authorization) removes your stored account credentials from the Keychain. Your training data remains on the device until you delete the App or clear its data.

**Your Choices and Rights**

You can:

- Deny or revoke location access in iOS Settings → imprUv → Location
- Revoke Sign in with Apple in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Sign-In & Security → Apps Using Apple ID
- Manage subscriptions in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
- Delete local data by deleting the App from your device
- Opt out of analytics: iOS 14.5+ users can limit tracking in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking (the App does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework for cross-app advertising)

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

**Children’s Privacy**
The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

**Data Security**
We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including secure on-device storage (Keychain, Core Data) and encrypted connections (HTTPS) when communicating with third-party services. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

**International Users**
If you use the App from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

**Changes to This Policy**
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy at the same URL and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
